Design exploration · not live

A look at a more dynamic design.

Two directions, each applied to the homepage and the services page, built on top of the current palette and content. Nothing here is on the live site — it's purely to compare the feel. Hover the cards, scroll the pages, and we'll cherry-pick what you like.

1 · In Motion

  • Scroll reveals
  • Staggered cards
  • Stat count-ups
  • Hero entrance
  • Gentle parallax

Keeps your exact look and feel, but brings it to life: sections and cards fade and rise as you scroll, the trust-bar numbers count up, the hero animates in, and the photo bands drift subtly. Lowest-risk way to feel more modern.

2 · Depth & Atmosphere

  • Gradient atmosphere
  • Glass cards
  • Fine grain
  • Image hover-zoom
  • Button glow

A more contemporary, premium feel: soft drifting gradient light behind the teal sections, a subtle film grain, glassy elevated cards with a hover accent, image zoom on the photos, and a glow on the primary buttons. Same palette, more depth.
